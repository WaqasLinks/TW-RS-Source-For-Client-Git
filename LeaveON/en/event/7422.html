Desert Jiu Jitsu Albuquerque Championship

The Desert Jiujitsu Championships is a SUBMISSIONS ONLY tournament! If there is no submissions during the round we run EBI overtime rounds.

The rules for the tournament:

Kids matches will be 5 minutes Submissions only!

Kids Brackets get guaranteed at least 2 matches

Teens and adults will be 10 minutes Submissions only!

Teens and adults are single elimination

Get your opponent to tap out within the round time and win that match. There will be matches that do not get a "tap out" within the round time. In that case we will go to over time (OT). We will do a sort of penalty, sudden death, (Like soccer) shootout. It will be put into 1 sudden death round for regular rounds and 3 overtime rounds for the finals.

Sudden Death Round: Athletes have a choice of back control or Armbar position from mount. The Athletes will be placed with one having the other's back with both hooks in and a seatbelt (over, under hands). They will try to finish their opponent while the other tries to escape. If that athlete finishes then we will set them the other way around and the other athlete will try to finish as well. If the second does not finish, they lose and first athlete wins. If the second does finish then the winner is the one with the fasted finishing time. Now if neither of the athletes finished the winner will be the one with the fastest escape time.

Divisions:

Kids Teens Adults

Kids Rules: The submissions allowed for kids will be as following. In case of any other submission attempts, the match will be stopped, child warned and will resume from same position if possible.

ONLY SUBMISSIONS ALLOWED: ARMBAR, LEG TRIANGLE, ARMTRIANGLE, KIMURA, AMERICANA/PAINTBRUSH, REAR NAKED CHOKE, GUILLOTINE, OMOPLATA, GOGOPLATA

Teens Rules: The Submissions allowed for teens will be as following. In case of any other submission attempt, the match will be stopped, teen will only recieve one warning, after that that teen will be disqualified.Match will resume from same position if possible.

ONLY SUBMISSIONS ALLOWED: ARMBAR, LEGTRIANGLE, ARMTRIANGLE, KIMURA, AMERICANA/PAINTBRUSH, REAR NAKED CHOKE, GUILLOTINE, OMOPLATA, GOGOPLATA, STRAIGHT ANKLE, KNEE BAR.

Adults Rules: The adults brackets will be seperated into two divisions. Beginners and Advanced. Beginners division all submissions are allowed except heel hooks and illegal neck crank. In case of an attempt of any of the illegal submissions the athlete will be warned once and reset from same position if possible. Advanced division all submissions are allowed.

NO REFUNDS
