Information

Pikes Peak Grappling presents Victory or Valhalla 2022 at the SoccerHaus in Colorado Springs on July 23rd, 2022.

Bigfoot Brawl 3 is a double elimination Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Tournament with belt and weight divisions for men, women, masters, teens, and kids, as well as absolute divisions for men, women, and teens.

Spectator and Coach's Passes can be purchased in advance at the time of registration or at the door the day of the event. 13 and up $15 Spectator ticket,  6-12 year olds are $10, and 5 and under are free. Gyms: for every five competitors (not divisions) your gym registers, you will receive one free Coach's Pass with a max of 3 coaches passes. Please don't add this pass to your cart. It will automatically be waiting for you at will-call the day of the event. 

Early Bird Registration – (Register before October 15th at 10:00 pm) $55 first division, $40 additional divisions

Late Registration – (Register before October 20th at 10:00 pm) $65 first division, $45 additional divisions

No Refunds, but grapplers may transfer their registration to another participant by Oct 19th.

*No Refunds/Comps will be given due to injury or illness*

All Registrations will need to be checked to make sure they are accurate by Oct 20th.  There will be NO changes made after October 20th to include school name registration and division updates. 

WEIGH-INS – Weigh-ins will be occurring on the evening of October 21st from 5-7pm. You will NOT need to wear your gi for weigh-ins, and there is a one-pound allowance for weight.

In Colorado Springs, weigh-ins will be at SoccerHaus 4845 List Dr, Colorado Springs, CO 80919.

In Pueblo, weigh-ins will be at Gracie Barra Pueblo 1306 N Main St Ste B, Pueblo, CO 81003

Out-of-town grapplers may weigh-in on the morning of the event. Day-of-tournament weigh-ins will take place from 7:30-8:15am at the SoccerHaus. ALL morning weigh-ins will need to be approved by Pikes Peak Grappling. If you cannot make the weigh-ins the evening before, please let us know by reaching out to us via Facebook messenger, Instagram or by email at [email protected].  

Doors Open @ 8:00am | Event Starts @ 9:00am

4845 List Dr. Colorado Springs, CO 80919


Check out our website at www.pikespeakgrappling.com for more information about the venue, parking, tournament rules, sponsorship and vendor opportunities, event merchandise, and more
