Information

THE SEASON FINALE IS THE CULMINATION OF THE 2022 NGL SEASON | THIS IS THE CHAMPIONSHIP EVENT!

WE WILL CROWN THE CHAMPIONS OF EACH WEIGHT DIVISION, GIVING MAXIMUM MATCHUPS TO ENSURE A QUALITY DAY WITH A DEFINITIVE CHAMPION. NO POINTS. JUST SUBMISSIONS!

.

We're going BIG for this one! With a bigger venue, bigger medals, and bigger prizes than ever before!  

.

Welcome in the new year with a goal in mind, to become NZ Submission Grappling Champion!

.

The Championship event comes around once a year so be sure to mark it in your calendar! We look forward to seeing you all after a good summer break with plenty of time to prepare.

.

Event details

Saturday FEBRUARY 11TH

Doors are open from 8:30am

Weigh-ins are open from 8.30am

Competition starts from 9.30am

.

Weight Divisions

Mens:

Featherweight - under 66kg

Lightweight - under 72kg

Welterweight - under 79kg

Middleweight - under 87kg

Light Heavyweight - under 95kg

Heavyweight - above 96kg

Womens:

Lightweight - under 60kg

Middleweight - under 70kg

Heavyweight - above 70kg

Kids, Youth and Teens:

Under 20kgs, 25kgs, 30kg, 35kgs, 40kgs, 45kgs, 50kgs, 55kgs, 60kgs, 65kgs, 70kgs, 75kgs, 80kgs, 85kgs, 90kgs, and 90kgs+

.

Ranking Divisions

White, Blue, Purple, Brown, & Black 

Note: If you are a pro MMA fighter or a black belt in another martial art, you are ineligible for the white belt division.

Medals will be presented shortly after divisions have finished. There won’t be any unnecessary or unwanted hanging around, the option to turn up, compete and go home will be there. 

NOTE* Gym owners and Head coaches will be invited for free. Contact via one of our social pages to make arrangements!

Kids/Youth/Teens Divisions

Ages 8-9:

White, Grey, Yellow, Orange+Green

Ages 10-12:

White, Grey, Yellow, Orange+Green

Ages 13-14:

White, Grey, Yellow, Orange+Green

Ages 15-17:

White, Grey+Yellow, Orange+Green+Blue

Please note: If there is a plus between belt divisions it means they will be merged together to ensure every competitor has a good amount of close in skill level matches.
