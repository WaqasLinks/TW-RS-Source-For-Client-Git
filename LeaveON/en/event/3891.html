BOP Open Adults' & Juvenile No-Gi Grappling Tournament

**Join us for the BOP Open Juvenile, Adult & Masteres No-Gi Grappling Tournament**

This is a Juvenile, Adult & Masters No-Gi Tournament open to all gyms and affiliates.

We will try our best to give everyone more than one match however this will be dependant on how many competitors in each division. Bracketing will be created based on number of competitors which will determine how many matches you have.

REGISTRATIONS:

Early Bird Registrations until 11:59pm Wednesday 31st of March - $45

Late Registrations: Thursday 1st of April - Wednesday 7th of April 11:59pm - $55

REGISTRATIONS CLOSE AT 11:59PM WEDNESDAY 7th OF APRIL 

NO LATE REGISTRATIONS will be accepted after this time. NO EXCEPTIONS! 

REGISTRATION IS CONFIRMED ONCE FULL PAYMENT HAS BEEN RECEIVED. 

RULES - SJJIF ruleset 

Please click here for a copy of the rules. 

MERGING 

We will take all safety preautions when merging brackets. 

Merging of divisions if no other competitors in their division, or where can’t be merged you will receive a coupon credit which can be used at one of our BOP Grappling Series Competitions. 

JUVENILE - Juveniles are aged 16-17 on day of tournament. We will not be accepting any juvenile registrations who are under 16years of age. No Exceptions.

Belts and/or weight classes may be merged due to the number of competitors entered. Safety measues will be adhered to when merging brackets as mentioned previously.

WEIGHT: Please ensure you are registered in the right weight class. Competitors over their registered weight will risk disqualification. You have until 11:59pm on Wednesday 7th of April to change/modify/cancel your registration.

UNIFORM: No-gi 

No loose clothing with pockets, zips, or buttons are permitted. Nails must be cut short and long hair tied back.

SPECTATORS: Gold coin donation which will be donated to Women's Refuge.

TOURNAMENT SCHEDULE:

9am: Weigh in

10:00am: First Matches Start

REFUNDS:

No cash refunds, however coupon credits issued by BOP Grapplers can be used at any of our tournaments.
