Information

The BCJJF Regional Championship will be a tournament for kids and adult of all skill levels.

The Regional Championship will be held in the following formats:

- IBJJF Ruleset for all kids divisions

 - IBJJF Ruleset in Gi

- Submission Only (or Referees Decision) Ruleset in Nogi **

                 **Heel hooks, Reaping, etc are allowed for Purple and above ADULT**                     **Heel hooks and reaping will not be allowed for Purple and above MASTERS (Kneebar and Toe hold are allowed)**

Open to ages 5 and up, all belt and skill levels.

Kids* and Juveniles are Gi and No Gi , double elimination.

*PARENTS PLEASE NOTE - All ages are determined by year. Meaning if your 7 year old turns 8 later this year AFTER the tournament, they will still compete as an 8 year old.

In an effort to create more matches for kids we will be doing custom weight classes which you can view when you go to sign up. Most divisions are 10 lbs apart except for the young kids (ages 4 & 5) and up to 15 lbs for the older larger children. 

All age groups are in sets of 2 years (example: 4 & 5, 6 & 7, 8 & 9 etc)

Adult and Master Gi  and Nogi Divisions.

Male Adult and Male Masters are single elimination.

Female Adult are double elimination.

Adult & Juvenile divisions will use IBJJF standard for weight classes

COACHING - Mat side coaching will be permitted for kids 8 & 9 and below. For kids 10+, juveniles, and adults,  coaching will be permitted but they must stay behind the border.

Weigh-ins are held the same day of the event.  Weigh ins for gi are done with the full gi and belt on. Kids are allowed a 2 pound allowance. Adults, Juveniles, and Masters must make weight or will be disqualified.

We will be using IBJJF weight classes for Juvenile and adult divisions

If you are competing in gi and no gi you need to register for the same weight class for both. You only weigh in one time and that is for your gi event. If you are only competing in no gi you will weigh-in in your no gi attire.

Blue belt and over Adults and Masters that medal in their division can sign up for the Absolute Division.

Tournament Start Time: 9am

Follow us on Instagram @bcjjf_ and find us on Facebook @bcjjf to stay up to date on all the lastest news before, during, and post tournament.
