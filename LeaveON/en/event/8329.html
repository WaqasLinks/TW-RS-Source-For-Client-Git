Information

Two day No Gi & Gi Tournament in Prague, Czech Republic, 2 guaranteed matches / Dvoudenní No Gi & Gi turnaj v Praze, 2 garantované zápasy

GENERAL INFORMATION

The event will take place in Prague, Czech Republic on September 24th (No Gi) and September 25th (Gi). 

Rules will be based on ADCC (for No Gi) and IBJJF (for Gi). 

We will provide ONE extra match for losers, but without chance to advance. So two matches during the event are guaranteed.

Time schedule

The venue will be open at 7:45

Registration and weight in 8:00-9:30

Rules explanation 9:45-10:00First matches 10:15

Expected finish 6:00 PM

No Gi Weight Classes

Men up to 65 kg; 70 kg; 76 kg; 83 kg; 91 kg; 100 kg; 100 kg +
Women up to 60 kg; 65 kg; 70kg; 70 kg +

Gi Weight Classes (with the uniform)

Men up to 64 kg; 70 kg; 76 kg; 82 kg; 88 kg; 94 kg; 101 kg; 101 kg +

Women up to 59 kg; 64 kg; 69 kg; 74 Kg; 74 kg +

There is only one opportunity to check the weight. If you don't make your weight you WILL be immediately disqualified and NO refund or voucher will be given! The weight tolerance will be set at 0,5 kg.

Minimum required age

Men 16 + years of age

Women 16 + years of age

years of age Experience level (No Gi)

  • Beginner (less than 2 years of experience in BJJ, Judo, Sambo, Wrestling, MMA)
  • Intermediate (2-4 years of experience) or blue belt in BJJ 
  • Professional (5 + years of experience) or purple belt in BJJ

Experience level (Gi)

  • White belts
  • Blue belts
  • Purple belts
  • Brown and Black belts together

REFUND POLICY

Cancelation received till September 14th, 11:59 PM - 100% money back

Cancelation after September 14th, 11:59 PM - 100% COUPON refund in case of illness or injury. Medical report needed!

Cancelation after September 21st, 11:59 PM - no refund back

OTHER INFORMATION

All fighters on first places will get nice medals and product gifts. 

Our sponsors are  SENS , SUPERION HERBS and HUHU CHILLI.
