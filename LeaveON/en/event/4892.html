AKA Open

August 21, 2021 Real Life Ministries - 1860 N Cecil Road, Post Falls, ID, 83854

Spectators:

$5 admission for anybody who is not competing. 

Registration: 

Register by August 17th at 11:59PM 

Single Division $65

Two Divisions $75

Absolute $20 Per Division

PRE-REGISTRATION IS REQUIRED! NO LATE REGISTRATIONS OR DAY OF REGISTRATIONS ALLOWED. 

Tentative Schedule: 

8:00AM Doors open Youth & Teen Weigh Ins

9:00AM Youth and Teen Matches Start 

10:00AM Adult Weigh Ins Begin 

11:00AM Adult Matches Start 

Please keep an eye on Smooth Comp Schedule for more detailed schedule updates! 

Rules: 

Youth and Teens:

IBJJF rule set for points; No Advantages Regular Division: 3 Minutes
White, Grey, Yellow Belts All Upper Body Submissions Allowed
Orange, Green, Blue Belts All Upper Body Submissions Allowed; Straight Ankle Locks Allowed
NOT Allowed for YOUTH and TEEN Jumping guard, Small joint manipulations ( fingers/ toes / wrists) , Slams, Twisting Leg Locks, Spinal or Neck Cranks.

Adults: 

IBJJF rule set for points; No Advantages Regular Division: 5 Minutes  Absolute Division: 8 Minutes 
Novice and White Belts All Upper Body Submissions Allowed, Straight Ankle Locks
Not Allowed Jumping Guard, Slams (Hard takedowns okay), Small Joint Manipulations (fingers and toes), Spinal or Neck Cranks
Blue Belts All Upper Body Submissions Allowed, Straight Ankle Locks, Wrist Locks. (NOGI ONLY) Toe Hold, Knee Bar
Not Allowed  Slams (Hard takedowns okay), Small Joint Manipulations (fingers and toes)
Purple, Brown, Black Belts  All Submissions Allowed; Heel Hooks ONLY in NOGI.
Not Allowed  Slams (Hard takedowns okay), Small Joint Manipulations (fingers and toes)

Divisions: 

Youth and Teens: 45 LBSand under, 55 LBS, 65 LBS, 75 LBS, 95 LBS, 105 LBS, 115 LBS, 125 LBS, 135 LBS, 145 LBS, 155 LBS, 165 LBS, 175 LBS, 185 LBS, 195 LBS, 205 LBS, 215 LBS, 216 + LBS

Adults: 115 LBS , 125 LBS, 135 LBS, 145 LBS, 155 LBS, 165 LBS, 175 LBS, 185 LBS, 195 LBS, 205 LBS, 215 LBS, 225 LBS, 235 + LBS

FORMAT:

Round Robin format for all regular youth, teen, and adult matches capped off at 10 competitors. If over 10 a new bracket will be created. This ensures all competitors will be able to have multiple matches. 

All absolute divisions will be single eliminations; with bronze match. 

Weigh Ins: 

Starting at 8:00AM Saturday morning. Only weighing in ONCE for all divisions. You may choose to weigh in WITHOUT Gi. YOU MUST WEIGH IN 60 MINUTES PRIOR TO DIVISION START TIME. 1LB Allowance. 

Brackets and Schedule:

Brackets and Schedule will be posted by Thursday August 19th, 2021 by 12:00PM. Available to view on Brackets and Schedule tab. During the event you will be able to veiw on any mobile device or screens at the event. 

Awards and Podium: 

1st (GOLD), 2nd (SLIVER), 3rd (BRONZE) place medals will be awarded at the event. We do not have a photographer at podium so remember to bring a camera!  

Results: 

Results are live streamed through smoothcomp. Click on the result tab to see. 

Registrations Edits / Credits / Refunds 

You have until registration closes (August 17, 2021 11:59PM) to make any registration edits. This includes weight changes, bracket changes, etc. You are able to make these changes within your own smoothcomp account by editing registrations. By canceling your registration you are able to receive a credit towards future event until August 17, 2021 11:59PM. By canceling your registration you can receive a refund until August 1, 2021 11:59PM. It is our strict policies to not extend past these dates. You can cancel you'r registration by logging into your own smoothcomp account.
