Information

The 2018 FNQ Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Championship is open to all junior and adult competitors for both Gi and No Gi.  It adheres to the IBJJF rules.  This is a competition which encourages players of any age to test their skill in order to improve their Jiu-Jitsu.
